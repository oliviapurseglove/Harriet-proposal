Why the questionnaire matters

The way you think about training matters as much as the training itself.

My dissertation looked at the psychology of sport and behaviour. One of the clearest things to come out of that research was this: the way someone trains tells you less about them than the way they think about training. How you relate to effort, how you handle the sessions that feel hard, what your relationship with your body has looked like over the years — all of that shapes what a programme needs to do and how it needs to be delivered.

After your first session I will send you a detailed questionnaire to sit alongside my own notes. It is not admin. It is the first piece of coaching. It can be completed in sections, takes around 30 to 40 minutes in total, and it changes what I write for you.
